Loving from afar

Distance is the biggest test in long distance relationships since it questions your trust towards your partner owing to the fact that you will be having questions about your partner's daily life. It makes you long for him/her but can't actually reach out to them physically. It tests patience due to the wait associated with calls, visits, surprises and the day you finally bridge the gap in between.it tests communication because words become your primary connection. And most importantly, it tests commitment because staying becomes a decision and not a feeling.

Red and green flags in relationships

Many people go into relationships without acknowledging their partner's behaviours. They seem to forget that red and green flags do exist and they determine most relationships. So, what are these flags? These so-called flags help many partners identify the level of seriousness of the relationship. They determine whether they are valued or not, he/she is the one or the backup plan of their partners and how people behave around them. Falling in love is the sweetest thing one can do but staying in it is what many have failed to do. Not because they don't want to but, they are so ignorant about the flags that exist in those relationships.
